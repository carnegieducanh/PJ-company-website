Repair

Pegatron has been repairing PCs and mobile devices since its establishment. We provide services which satisfy our customers through accurate diagnosis by experienced skilled domestic and oveseas technicians. Components are appropriately and speedily changed, and assurance of repair quality is done with dedicated test tools.

Kitting

Kitting is the process of installing software on new PCs and mobile devices at the request of customers. The kitting process involves installing the OS and necessary software add-ons, and setting up the connection to the company network. This allows the user to turn on the PC and immedediately start using it.

LCM service
